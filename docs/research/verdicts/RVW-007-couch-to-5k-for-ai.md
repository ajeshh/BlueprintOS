# RVW-007 — "Couch-to-5K for AI" (progressive daily habit; smallest next step)

## The claim
- **Source:** Lenny's Newsletter — Hilary Gridley. `docs/research/inbox/couch-to-5k-for-ai-habit.md`
- **Core assertion:** *Build AI skill the way Couch-to-5K builds running — a progressive daily
  practice (<10 min/day), smallest-possible-first-step; "getting started is everything," overwhelm is
  the failure mode. Foundations (delegation, breaking down work, quality bar) outlast tools; the real
  payoff is expanded ambition, not productivity.*

## Rubric
| # | Question | Finding |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Contradicts a PRINCIPLE? | **The philosophy aligns** with #2 (JIT, smallest earned step). **But the *mechanic* (daily streak / habit program) collides** with the canvas anti-metrics (no gamification — proven in the v0.36 board work) and R&H #1/#5 (BOSS bloating; cumulative pressure on founders). Split decision: principle yes, streak no. |
| 2 | Evidence grade | **Highest in the batch.** Named, respected behavior-change practitioner (WHOOP, Dropbox, Big Health; clinically-validated depression outcomes), well-established science (Pychyl, smallest-step). **Independent of the Hamza corpus.** The verdict turns on scope-fit + humane, not evidence. |
| 3 | Duplicate or sharpen? | BOSS's JIT/smallest-step philosophy **already exists** (PRINCIPLE #2, `/welcome`). The "smallest next step" *framing* could **sharpen** `/welcome` + the conscience's beginner-cohort nudges. A habit-*program* is not BOSS's shape (build-incubator, not a learn-AI course). |
| 4 | Who serves / harms? | Serves beginner cohorts (`first-product`/`vibe-coder-newbie`/`non-tech-founder`) via gentle progressive onboarding. **Harm risk = a daily nag/streak** — the dopamine/pressure trap the conscience itself warns against. |
| 5 | Cost / ceremony | The *philosophy* is free. A streak/habit **loop** would be exactly the gamification ceremony BOSS refuses. |

## Verdict: ADAPT

Take the **principle** (smallest-next-step / progressive, overwhelm-is-the-enemy) as a framing for
`/welcome` and the conscience's beginner-cohort nudges — and **explicitly reject the daily-streak /
habit-program mechanic**. The modification: behavior-change *framing* in; streak/dopamine *loop* out.

## If ADOPT / ADAPT
- **What's modified from the claim:** keep "break it into the smallest first step, name the one next
  action"; drop "daily streak," "30-day program," any tracking/gamification. BOSS nudges the *next
  step*, never a *cadence to maintain*.
- **What to do (scope-gated):** founder-facing — a `/welcome` + conscience beginner-cohort framing
  ("here's the single smallest next step"). The actionable surface is founder-facing, so the build is
  queued with the founder-facing work, not done now.
- **⚠️ Guardrail recorded:** a future session may be tempted to "add a daily AI-habit streak to
  BOSS." **Don't** — RVW-007 already ruled the streak mechanic out on humane grounds (#6 + R&H). This
  is the institutional-memory value of the verdict.
