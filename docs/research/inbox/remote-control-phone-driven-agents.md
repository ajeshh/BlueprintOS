# Claim: drive your agents from your phone via /remote-control — "be a CEO all day"

An enthusiastic personal account of Claude Code's `/remote-control`.

## Core assertion (the load-bearing claim)

*Use `/remote-control` — enter it in a CLI or Desktop session and it syncs in real time to the phone
Claude app, so you can keep 2-3 agents on task from anywhere (meetings, gym, walks). You become "a
CEO all day while your agents do the grunt work," staying "plugged in" without being at your desk,
and you can pause agents for days and resume at will (with an always-on setup like a Mac mini).*

Claimed benefits:
- **Mobility / freedom** — work from meetings, gym, walks; answer agents' questions via phone voice
  mode; "plug out" for days and pick up where you left off.
- **Wellbeing** — taking more walks, gym way more, wants a dog / to be outside; "feels insanely
  better."
- **Replaced doomscrolling** — deleted social media; checking phone for agent updates gives "that
  same weird dopamine hit," so no urge to doomscroll; reads Kindle morning/night instead.
- **Governance** — can spot bugs/flaws in the data and fix them in real time from the phone.

The framing: "literally an earpiece and that's it… agents report back via voice… you are basically a
CEO all day." "I honestly think this is going to be the future."

## Tensions to pressure-test at vet time (my read — NOT the verdict)

- **The humane lens is the whole ballgame here (PRINCIPLE #6 + R&H #1).** The post's own evidence
  cuts both ways: it celebrates swapping the doomscroll compulsion for an *agent-update* dopamine hit,
  and "always plugged in, working at the gym." Is that humane (reclaimed attention, more movement) or
  a *new* attention trap dressed as freedom — the exact "BOSS becomes the thing it warns against"
  failure? `mentor-humane` has override authority; this is its kind of question.
- **Scope / relevance.** `/remote-control` is a *Claude Code harness feature*, not a BOSS practice.
  Does BOSS adopt anything at all, or is this out-of-scope (a host capability BOSS doesn't own —
  IDEA-006 host-contract territory)? Most likely BOSS doesn't "adopt" it; the live question is whether
  it informs a humane stance / a mentor-humane note about phone-driven multi-agent working patterns.
- **Evidence grade.** n=1, very recent, pure enthusiasm ("life changing," "this is the future"), zero
  outcome data — and a wellbeing claim (deleted social media, reading more) made days in, with obvious
  novelty effect. Low grade for anything beyond "one person's first weeks felt good."
- **Verify the feature claim itself.** Before vetting, confirm `/remote-control` exists and works as
  described (real-time CLI/Desktop ↔ phone sync, voice) — the claim is secondhand and breathless;
  don't vet a feature that isn't what the post says it is.

## Why it's worth keeping in the queue

It's the sharpest test yet of BOSS's *own* humane principle against a genuinely seductive workflow —
the kind of thing the conscience exists to be skeptical of. Even a REJECT/NOT-YET here is valuable as
recorded reasoning about what BOSS will and won't endorse about always-on agent work.
