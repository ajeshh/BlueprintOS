# Founder-recognition + recruiting surface (NOT founder-facing personas)

## Occasion
- 2026-06-02 — While planning wayfinding ([[IDEA-018]]), Ajesh proposed building out **founder-facing
  personas** — one per critical user type — to serve **triple duty**: (1) wayfinding (self-ID → find
  your path through BOSS), (2) reusable marketing material (self-ID → see the value), (3) a
  self-understanding mirror ("a way to categorize or help someone understand themselves"). Convened
  **mentor-humane** (override authority on dignity) + **mentor-gtm** (positioning). They converged
  hard — a rare humane-and-viable alignment — and the convergence reshaped the idea.

## The decision (what the two lenses converged on)
**Unbundle the triple duty.** One job is earned; two are not. And the bundling is the hazard — it lets
the unearned jobs ride in on the earned one.

| Duty | Verdict | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Wayfinding** (self-ID → route) | **Build** (already in [[IDEA-018]] as persona = entry filter) | Humane-positive *as routing*: a turnstile, not a verdict on the person. |
| **Marketing** (self-ID → value) | **Already exists — do NOT rebuild as personas** | `positioning-pass-001.md` §8's 8 cohort one-liners are the safe version: *situations, not identities.* |
| **Self-understanding mirror** | **Defer — not earned** | A self-knowledge claim BOSS isn't entitled to make at n=0; reflects Ajesh's assumptions, not real founders. |

## The reframe (load-bearing — this is the whole idea now)
**From "founder-facing personas" → "a founder-recognition + recruiting surface."** The canvas v0.3
riskiest assumption is **demand (n=0 real users), and BOSS has spent ~22 releases "building around the
risk."** A polished triple-duty persona artifact is *more* building-around-the-risk — and the *pull to
craft the beautiful mirror IS that instinct* (BOSS's own conscience would flag it; the right response
is `/canvas`, not more surface). **But pointed at recruiting instead of decoration**, the persona
tensions become the cheapest instrument BOSS has to *end* the n=0 problem: self-ID as **qualification,
not conversion.** It turns the biggest under-used asset (8 sharp tensions) into the experiment the
canvas says is the only thing that matters, and answers positioning §13's open "does 'thinking layer'
land?" for free.

## The one rule both lenses stated independently
**Describe the SITUATION, never the PERSON.**
- ✅ *"Half-finished projects pile up faster now that building is cheap"* — a world the reader lives in.
- ❌ *"You're a vibe-virtuoso who ships nothing"* — a verdict on the reader (= shame-as-funnel).
- The shame version doesn't just harm — it **repels** the target cohort (anti-growth-hacking is the
  differentiator; a manufactured-inadequacy page reads as "a growth-hack tool in a calm sweater"). So
  humane-positive and viable point the same way.

## Non-negotiable design constraints (OVERRIDES — humane beats viable)
1. **Situation, never person.** Every founder-facing line names a world the reader lives in, never a
   verdict on them. The load-bearing rule; the rest enforce it.
2. **Founder-led ID; BOSS never assigns identity.** The founder reads options and picks "that's me."
   BOSS may *route* on a picked cohort; it may never *tell* a founder who they are. **The cohort label
   is never the subject of a conscience moment** (the conscience fires on *project state*, never on the
   founder's character — that would be the BOSS.DK alter-ego punching down in a real work moment).
3. **Turnstile, not tattoo.** The persona is disposable after it routes you; no label persists as a
   lens BOSS views the founder through during their work.
4. **No clinical-note leak.** Nothing from the internal `persona-*.md` diagnostic bodies (e.g.
   `persona-vibe-virtuoso.md`: "shipped exactly zero products," "you secretly believe…") may appear in
   any founder-facing surface, even paraphrased. Internal personas and founder-facing surfaces are
   **different artifacts with different voices.**
5. **3–4 pace-groups, never 8 character-types** (extends [[IDEA-018]]'s decision to all founder-facing
   persona surfaces). 8 named characters = both R&H #1 matrix-bloat *and* the shame-funnel risk ×8.
6. **Zero BOSS-coined terms** on any founder-facing surface (no *conscience / modes / loops / cohorts /
   moments*). If a tension can't be said in the founder's own words, it isn't ready to be public. (GTM
   lens's hard rule — also the cure for the "too inside" problem; personas *force* founder-language.)

## Smallest version (when/if built — deliberately deferred)
- **One page / README section / Gist. 3 tensions, not 8** (reuse IDEA-018's 3–4 pace-group collapse),
  each a §8-style before/after tension in pure founder language.
- **CTA is contact, not signup:** *"If one of these made you wince — I'm looking for 3 founders to
  watch use this for 20 minutes."* Success metric = **conversations booked**, not pageviews.
- Ship where the cohort already is (build-in-public timelines); a post or Gist is enough to test the
  line — don't build a landing-page apparatus.
- **What the result teaches:** "that's me, what is it?" → the wedge works, the *conversation* (not the
  page) tells you whether to invest more. Shrug/can't-tell → tensions are over-fit to Ajesh (Risk #6 /
  IDEA-009 cheerleading-bias made real); fix the tensions, don't build more surface. Everyone-leans-in
  → too broad (Godin); sharpen until the wrong founder scrolls past.

## Open questions / re-open trigger
- **Re-open trigger:** the day Ajesh is ready to *recruit* (not decorate) — i.e. willing to take the
  20-minute calls. Until then this stays *exploring*. The build is hours, not a sprint; the gating
  thing is the readiness to do founder contact, which is the canvas's actual ask.
- **Self-understanding mirror:** revisit *only after* watching ≥3 real founders react. "A mirror you
  hand someone before watching a single real founder look into one reflects your assumptions, not
  them." Staleness/over-fit twin of [[IDEA-009]].
- **Watched-harm for the next `/canvas` pass (flagged, not yet written — canvas has unrelated in-flight
  edits):** add an R&H entry *"founder-facing persona shame-funnel"* so the dark version can't sneak
  back in. (mentor-humane's recommendation.)
- **Don't grow a second persona vocabulary.** The runtime "cohorts" (`.boss/config.json`, cohort-aware
  conscience, `persona-*` agents) are an internal instrument ([[IDEA-009]]: synthetic shrinks as real
  grows, never reverse). A public portrait set is the most expensive synthetic guess to walk back —
  reuse the pace-group collapse, never re-expand to 8 public portraits.
